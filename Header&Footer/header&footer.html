He is a character from Pallet Town in the Pokémon universe. Gary is one of the main rivals of the protagonist, Ash Ketchum, in the Pokémon anime and games. In the Pokémon series, Pallet Town is the hometown of Ash, and it's where he starts his journey to become a Pokémon Trainer. Gary Oak, being Professor Oak's grandson, also hails from Pallet Town and begins his own Pokémon journey at the same time as Ash.
